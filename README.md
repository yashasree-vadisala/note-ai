# 📚 NoteAI – AI-Powered Learning Assistant

NoteAI is an AI-powered learning platform that transforms educational YouTube videos into structured learning resources using Google Gemini AI. The platform helps students learn efficiently by automatically generating study notes, quizzes, deep analytical questions, AI-powered tutoring, and progress analytics.


## 🚀 Features

### 📝 AI Notes Generation
- Generate concise and structured study notes from YouTube videos.
- Extracts key concepts and important points.
- Supports quick learning and revision.

### 🎯 AI Quiz Generation
- Automatically generates Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
- Instant quiz evaluation and scoring.
- Helps students assess their understanding.

### 🧠 Deep Questions
- Generates higher-order thinking and scenario-based questions.
- Encourages critical thinking and concept application.
- Supports deeper learning beyond memorization.

### 🤖 NoteAI Tutor
- AI-powered chatbot for personalized learning assistance.
- Answers student queries based on the generated content.
- Provides concept explanations and learning support.

### 📊 Progress Analytics
- Tracks student learning activities.
- Displays quiz scores and learning consistency.
- Visualizes overall learning progress.

### 📑 AI Presentation Generator
- Generates structured presentation content from study notes.
- Helps students prepare presentations quickly.

🔄 Workflow

### Step 1
User enters an educational YouTube video URL.

### Step 2
Backend validates the URL and extracts the YouTube Video ID.

### Step 3
The transcript is fetched using the YouTube Transcript API.

### Step 4
The transcript is cleaned and processed.

### Step 5
Google Gemini AI analyzes the content.

### Step 6
The AI generates:
- Study Notes
- Quiz
- Deep Questions
- AI Tutor Responses
- Presentation Content

### Step 7
Generated content is displayed through the frontend dashboard.
